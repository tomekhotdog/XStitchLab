# XStitchLab Style Guide (v1.1)
## Architectural Miniatures Series

---

## 1. Canvas

**Standard grid**

- 40 × 40 stitches

**Rationale**

- Recognisable landmarks
- Quick stitch time (~4–6 hours)
- Fits small hoops

---

## 2. Fabric

**Recommended**

- 14ct Aida

**Finished size**

| Grid | Fabric | Finished Size |
|-----|------|------|
| 40 × 40 | 14ct | ~7.3 × 7.3 cm |
| 40 × 40 | 16ct | ~6.3 × 6.3 cm |

**Kit fabric**

- Minimum: 10 × 10 cm
- Preferred: 12 × 12 cm

---

## 4. Outline Rules

**Thread**

- DMC 310 (Black)

**Technique**

- Backstitch
- 1 strand

**Used for**

- Building outlines
- Windows
- Roof edges
- Doors
- Structural definition

---

## 5. Stitch Structure

**Main design**

- Full cross stitch
- 2 strands

**Outlines**

- Backstitch
- 1 strand

---

## 6. Window Design Rules

Keep windows readable at small scale.

| Window Type | Stitch Size |
|-----|-----|
| Small window | 1 × 1 |
| Arched window | 2 × 2 |
| Large window | 2 × 3 |

**Maximum**

- 3 × 3 stitches

---

## 7. Architectural Simplification

**Remove**

- Brick textures
- Carvings
- Statues
- Signage
- Cables
- Plants
- People

**Preserve**

- Overall silhouette
- Window rhythm
- Roofline
- Landmark feature

## 9. Background

**Default**

- DMC 3865 Winter White

**Guideline**

- Let fabric act as sky/background
- Avoid stitching large background areas

## 11. Kit Contents

Standard kit includes:

- 14ct Aida (12 × 12 cm)
- Pre-cut thread (8 colours)
- Size 24 needle
- Printed pattern
- Colour legend
- Stitch instructions

**Optional**

- Mini wooden hoop

---

## 12. Pattern Layout

Pattern PDF should contain:

1. Cover image
2. Finished design preview
3. Thread legend
4. Colour + symbol chart
5. Backstitch diagram
6. Stitch instructions
