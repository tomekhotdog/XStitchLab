- Tall, slender stone tower rising from the left side of the building

ROOF:

- Long red or terracotta tiled roof

- Pale stone or beige main building

SIMPLIFICATION RULES:

- NO stone texture or brick patterns

- NO tiny decorative carvings

- NO people, trees, cars, market stalls, or surrounding buildings

- Focus on the silhouette: long hall + arches + tall tower



STYLE:

- Cute, charming, slightly stylised pixel art

- Bold, clean shapes

- Strong dark brown or dark grey outlines (not pure black)

- Flat colour blocks only

- No gradients, lighting effects, or anti-aliasing



CROSS-STITCH CONSTRAINTS:

- Design must fit within a 40 x 40 stitch grid

- Maximum 8 total colours

- High contrast between architectural elements

- Clear separation between roof, walls, arches, and tower

1. Pale beige / warm stone (main building)

2. Very light grey (stone variation)
