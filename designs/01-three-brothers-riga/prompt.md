Create a pixel art illustration designed specifically to become a cross-stitch pattern.

Subject: The “Three Brothers” houses in Riga, Latvia — three medieval townhouses standing side by side, front-facing, tightly connected in a row.

CRITICAL PROPORTION RULES (must be followed exactly):
- ALL THREE BUILDINGS MUST BE THE SAME TOTAL HEIGHT from ground to roof peak
- The LEFT building must be NARROW — approximately HALF THE WIDTH of each of the other two buildings
- Despite being narrower, the left building must NOT be shorter

BUILDING DETAILS:

LEFT HOUSE (green, narrowest but FULL HEIGHT):
- Half the width of the middle and right houses
- SAME HEIGHT as the other two buildings
- Soft muted sage or pale green facade
- Simple triangular gable roof
- THREE clear rows of small windows above the door
- Windows vertically aligned and evenly spaced
- Simple, modest appearance

MIDDLE HOUSE (beige, decorative and symmetrical):
- Full width (about double the width of the left house)
- SAME HEIGHT as the other two
- Warm beige / creamy yellow facade
- Curved decorative gable top
- Strong vertical symmetry
- Even rows of windows on upper floors
- Ground floor must include windows on BOTH sides of the central door (no blank wall sections)

RIGHT HOUSE (white, stepped gable):
- Same width as the middle house
- SAME HEIGHT as the other two
- White or very light cream facade
- Distinct stepped gable roof silhouette
- Evenly spaced dark windows in rows
- Simple door at ground level

Shared architectural features:
- Red or terracotta roof edging
- Dark brown window openings (not black)
- Simple dark doors
- No street, pavement, people, signage, or background clutter

STYLE:
- Cute, charming, slightly stylised pixel art
- Bold, readable shapes
- Strong dark brown outlines (not pure black)
- Windows simplified to small dark rectangles
- No bricks, textures, or tiny decorative details

CROSS-STITCH CONSTRAINTS:
- Entire design must fit within a 30 x 30 stitch grid
- Maximum 8 total colours
- Flat colour blocks only
- No gradients, no shading, no anti-aliasing
- High contrast between adjacent areas
- Simple very light sky blue background

COLOUR PALETTE (max 8):
1. Muted sage / pale green (left house)
2. Warm beige / creamy yellow (middle house)
3. White / very light cream (right house)
4. Terracotta / muted red (roof accents)
5. Dark brown (outlines, windows, doors)
6. Medium brown (door or roof variation)
7. Very light blue (background)
8. Optional soft grey-beige accent

OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS:
- Clean pixel art only
- Centered composition
- No text, labels, borders, or mockups
- Do NOT show fabric or embroidery
